Appdock is an educational and informational platform for people who wish to further or simply aquire knowledge based on technology. It provides informational how-to's on how ot install mobile aplications as well as how to use them.

For developers, it provides an informative platform to test their apps in a live setting as well as distribute their apps and gather information on how to improve on them based on localized feedback.
